Thus I joined a gym mainly focusing on cardio and diet and doing my work strictly and honestly I started seeing the changes, though they were not physical but still I was loosing about 1-2 kgs a week. At that time I realised that if you do your job with complete honesty you will see the change. I continued the gym for about 5 weeks and lost a total a 9 kgs, that brought a huge change in me but still I was very far from being fit. I continued with my diet and played soccer in college. But it didn't help me as I was overweight and all those soccer drills were just making me injury prone, at that time I didn't realised it but all those crampful nights made me realised it. So I stopped playing soccer and was help from my brother(who is also a runner), and he too told me the samething. So as a solutiion to it he asked me to start running and I also took it seriously.
