My First Run

December 3, 5:30 pm , I was all set for this journey. I was ready with my shoes tied and was ready for the run. I thought that it is very easy 4-5 kms and I can do it very easily, I had no idea about my pace, my cadence nothing, I was just running and there I go after 500m all drained out so I walked a few metres and then sprinted my way through, again walked and sprinted this went for a total of 1.73kms and then I stopped. I was disappointed, disheartened and felt baffled, and thought I was never going to make it, I would never be fit again. But next evening I again went for it, but the result was same no change , I covered 1.86 kms. Next day was also the same.

And the next day I said to myself "I have to live my whole life like this a 88 kg boy who was worthless" and I stopped running.
