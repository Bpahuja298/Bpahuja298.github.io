Getting back at it

For two months I didn't did any running just played soccer in college, knowing the consequences it can have on my overweighted body. But after the annual sports fest of the college, I was determined to again start running and never quitting or even stopping it.

With only one goal in mind though you run slow, you have to run long, you need cover those miles, you will not stop until you had done what you have decided for the day. I was going good and in the span of 5 weeks I was able to cover a total of 88 kms, in which I did many 5k runs and also my longest run of 6km.
